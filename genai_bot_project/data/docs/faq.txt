FAQ - Company Help

Q: How can I reset my password?
A: Go to the settings page and click "Forgot Password." Follow the email link sent to you.

Q: What are your customer support hours?
A: Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM.

Q: How can I update my account information?
A: Log in and go to "My Profile" → "Edit Details" → Save.
